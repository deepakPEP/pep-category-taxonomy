SECTION 3: SUBCATEGORY RULES — STRICTLY ENFORCED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

RULE 1 — SUBCATEGORY NAMES MUST BE GLOBALLY UNIQUE:
Each subcategory name must appear under EXACTLY ONE category in the entire taxonomy.
WRONG: "Cold Chain Logistics & Storage" appearing under both Agriculture AND Machinery
RIGHT: "Cold Chain Logistics & Storage" → Agriculture & Food Products only

RULE 2 — NEVER USE CATEGORY NAMES AS SUBCATEGORY NAMES:
WRONG subcategories: "Machinery & Equipment", "Software & IT Solutions", "Services", "Raw Materials"
These are category names. Do not use them as subcategories.

RULE 3 — SUBCATEGORIES MUST DESCRIBE PRODUCT TYPES, NOT BUSINESS ENTITIES:
WRONG: "Garment Manufacturers (OEM/ODM)", "Fabric Traders", "Exporters & Buying Houses"
RIGHT: "Men's Wear", "Fabrics", "Garment Accessories & Trims"

RULE 4 — EACH SUBCATEGORY MUST CONTAIN MULTIPLE PRODUCTS:
Never create a subcategory for just one or two products.
Merge narrow subcategories into broader ones.

RULE 5 — NO DUPLICATE SUBCATEGORY NAMES WITH DIFFERENT FORMATTING:
WRONG: "Women's personal care products" AND "Women's Personal Care Products"
WRONG: "Over-the-Counter Medicines" AND "Over-The-Counter Medicines"
WRONG: "Ethnic & Cultural Clothing" AND "Ethnic And Cultural Clothing"
Always use Title Case with & (ampersand), not "And".

RULE 6 — SERVICES BELONG IN SERVICES & SUPPORT:
Do not put service subcategories under product categories.
WRONG: "Poultry Farming Services" under Agriculture
RIGHT: Move to Services & Support
